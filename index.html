1. Purpose of the Project

The Partners agree to combine their skills, efforts, and resources to collaborate honestly and diligently on a shared game development project. Both parties enter this partnership in good faith, with the ultimate objective of engineering a high-quality, successful, and professional digital product through equal dedication, mutual respect, and creative cooperation.

2. Covenant of Honesty and Transparency

Both Partners explicitly pledge to maintain absolute integrity, honesty, and transparency throughout the entire lifecycle of the project. This commitment strictly includes, but is not limited to, the following behaviors:

  • Open Communication: Sharing technical progress, development blockers, design challenges, and timeline realities truthfully, without withholding critical project data or project files.
  • Asset Accountability: Ensuring that all code base repositories, scripts, visual assets, audio elements, and layout designs contributed to the project are fully original works or are explicitly acquired via appropriate legal channels.
  • No Plagiarism: Neither partner shall copy, pirate, or steal intellectual work from external individual creators or commercial studios, nor claim individual credit for efforts that were produced collaboratively.

3. Legal and Regulatory Compliance

The Partners explicitly agree to operate the project in strict accordance with all applicable intellectual property and digital standard laws:

  • Intellectual Property (IP): All third-party development tools, game engines, specialized plugins, open-source modules, and commercial media libraries used during production must strictly comply with their respective licensing structures and Terms of Service.
  • Data and Privacy: If the software configuration collects, tracks, or transmits any form of user metrics or telemetry data, development frameworks must adhere strictly to current digital data privacy rules.
  • Platform Distribution: Any eventual commercial execution, marketing distribution, or monetization of the final software deployment must fundamentally align with the official publishing and legal policies of the distribution store platforms utilized.

4. Work Ethic and Mutual Contribution

  • Each partner promises to fulfill their assigned task assignments and development metrics with strict professional accountability.
  • Neither partner shall intentionally stall progress, delay critical code deployment, or knowingly compromise the build quality of the digital system through negligence or deliberate absence.
